# Complete Math Roadmap
**Canadian Curriculum + ML/CS Math — Restructured**

> **Structure:** Learn all math during summers. Do all coding learning during school years.

---

## Golden Rules

1. School carries the curriculum. Use self-study time for CS math school skips.
2. Linear algebra before calculus — no calculus needed to start it.
3. Simulate, don't just calculate. Verify every stats/probability concept in Python the same day.
4. The chain rule is backprop. Spend more time here than anything else in calculus.
5. MML book is your capstone math text (free at [mml-book.github.io](https://mml-book.github.io)). Read it in Gr. 12 once school has given you the calculus foundation.
